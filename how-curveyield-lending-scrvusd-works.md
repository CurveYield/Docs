# How CurveYield Lending scrvUSD Works

CurveYield’s premier service is a V2 Morpho vault that manages deposits of scrvUSD and issues cylscrvUSD as the vault token. cylscrvUSD represents a share of the vault and increases in value over time as yield is accrued.

The vault allocates deposited capital across a set of curated lending and borrowing markets. These markets use scrvUSD as the lending asset and accept yield-bearing Curve ecosystem assets as collateral.

Borrowers deposit collateral and borrow scrvUSD from these markets, potentially leveraging their yield-bearing positions to increase returns. The interest paid on these loans is returned to the vault and distributed to cylscrvUSD holders.

cylscrvUSD earns yield from both the intrinsic yield of scrvUSD and borrower interest from the lending markets, with both sources compounding together.

**This structure allows the same capital to generate yield from multiple sources simultaneously, increasing overall capital efficiency.**<br>
