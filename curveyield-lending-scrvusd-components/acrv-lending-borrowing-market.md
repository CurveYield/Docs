# aCRV -  Lending/Borrowing Market

Underlying Asset: [aCRV (Aladdin Concentrator)](https://concentrator.aladdin.club/): a vault token representing a position in [cvxCRV (Convex CRV Staking)](https://curve.convexfinance.com/stake?utm_source=chatgpt.com) that automatically compounds yield.

cvxCRV is a liquid derivative of veCRV that earns yield from Curve trading fees, CRV emissions, and protocol rewards while remaining liquid.

Market: [acrv Market](https://app.morpho.org/ethereum/market/0x5a9a736feaefa36d605af46ca54ee1640b127285e9763e9668dd91b1c91bca28/acrv-scrvusd)
