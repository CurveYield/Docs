# asdCRV Lending/Borrowing Market

Underlying Asset: [asdCRV (Aladdin Concentrator)](https://concentrator.aladdin.club/): a vault token representing a position in [sdCRV (StakeDAO CRV Locker)](https://www.stakedao.org/lockers/crv?utm_source=chatgpt.com) that automatically compounds yield.

sdCRV is a liquid locker token received when CRV is deposited into StakeDAO and locked as veCRV, providing yield from trading fees, CRV emissions, and vote incentives.

asdCRV builds on sdCRV by applying veSDT boost through StakeDAO’s Liquid Locker system, increasing voting power and vote incentive earnings, which are then compounded by the vault.

Market: [asdCRV Market](https://app.morpho.org/ethereum/market/0x7832d00f0294515e97ccdbe10282dd768b4c016d8ee4a8e7ad29b6cdcc167a4d/asdcrv-scrvusd#overview)
