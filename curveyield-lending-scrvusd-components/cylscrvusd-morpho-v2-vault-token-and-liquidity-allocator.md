# cy-scrvUSD - Euler Earn Vault Token and Liquidity Allocator

CurveYield scrvUSD is a Euler Earn vault share token representing scrvUSD tokens that are distributed into curated lending markets, where they are lent to borrowers against their collateral.

cy-scrvUSD is a liquid, yield-bearing derivative that can be deposited into liquidity pools to earn trading fees (like wstETH) while also accruing lending yield and scrvUSD’s intrinsic yield.

scrvUSD is deposited into markets as the lending asset, and borrower interest is returned to the vault and compounded, increasing the value of CurveYield Lending scrvUSD over time.

Token: cy-scrvUSD Token (Etherscan)
